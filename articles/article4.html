Understanding Layer 2 Scaling Solutions for Ethereum

By Alex Johnson | July 20, 2025 · 12 min read

Ethereum has faced scalability issues for years — but Layer 2 solutions are changing the game. Rollups, sidechains, and state channels are driving faster, cheaper transactions while preserving Ethereum's security and decentralization.

Optimistic and zk-Rollups are now widely adopted, with protocols like Arbitrum, Optimism, and zkSync leading the charge. These technologies batch transactions off-chain and settle on-chain, drastically reducing gas fees and congestion.

For developers and users alike, understanding Layer 2 is essential for navigating the next evolution of decentralized applications.
